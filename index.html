About OMNi

More Than A Pickup Group

OMNi started as a soccer community and is growing into a full experience. Right now, we bring people together through weekly pickup, occasional leagues, tournaments, and social events across The Woodlands, Conroe, Tomball, and North Houston.

The long-term goal is to create a permanent OMNi home where people can come to play, socialize, train, recover, and get their workout on.
