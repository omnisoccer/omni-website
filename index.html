About OMNi

Built For Players. Powered By Community.

OMNi Soccer Community brings adults together through weekly pickup soccer, leagues, tournaments, and events across North Houston.

Whether you're stepping onto the field for the first time or you've been playing for years, OMNi is a place to compete, connect, stay active, and belong.

The Vision

Tomorrow, We’re Building Something Bigger.

OMNi started with one goal: to bring people together through soccer. Today, we're building North Houston's soccer community through weekly pickup, leagues, tournaments, and events.

Tomorrow, we're building a permanent home where people can play, train, recover, compete, and connect.

⚽ Indoor Soccer

Year-round pickup, leagues, and tournaments in a dedicated facility.

💪 Fitness Center

Strength and conditioning for soccer players and everyday athletes.

🧊 Recovery Zone

Recovery, wellness, stretching, cold plunge, and sauna concepts.

🏆 Leagues & Tournaments

Competitive and social play for multiple levels and formats.

🎓 Training Programs

Skills development, private coaching, and fitness-based training.

☕ Community Hub

A place to meet teammates, watch matches, and build lasting connections.
